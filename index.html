Hi, I'm Ava.

I'm a graduate of Purdue University, earning a Bachelor of Arts in Public Relations and Strategic Communication. Throughout my academic journey, I have immersed myself in various professional roles that have honed my expertise in curating diverse creative content. This encompasses a wide spectrum from video marketing, brand development, copywriting, and social media strategy.

Currently based in Los Angeles, I serve as a Social Media Coordinator working in the B2B space at Impact Networking where I've been entrusted with the pivotal task of strengthening the brand's narrative, growing their social presence, and generating new leads. However, I am versed in B2C as well and some of the notable brands I have contributed to and grown are PLEZi (by Michelle Obama), DON'T QUIT! (Body By Jake with Damar Hamlin as a partner), and Daily Crunch (an award-winning Women Owned brand).

I am actively seeking opportunities in social media and digital marketing where I can leverage my creative mind and embrace new challenges. My passion is to fuse strategic thinking with creative expression, driving tangible results for brands and organizations.
